## PR & Communication

![](/images/wings/pr-communication/PR-Communication-1.svg)

PR and Communication Wing- The wing of the Eloquents. This particular wing of UDAF strives to create relationships with external parties as well as internal parties by communicating with them in the smartest way possible. It aids UDAF in many ways, such as managing sponsorships, inviting guests, and receiving them cordially in every event arranged by UDAF. Not only that, the wing also operates the social media platforms that represent UDAF to the mass people.

People in the PR and Communication wing are smart, they know how to talk and influence people. And when they are surrounded by their experienced wing members, they become more adept in it. In addition to that, the wing plays a huge role in improving their professional life communication skills as the members have to regularly communicate with people through face to face conversations, letters, emails, phone calls and other media. 

To be precise, those who join this fabulous wing not only get a chance to represent UDAF directly but also have a great opportunity to improve their communication skills.
