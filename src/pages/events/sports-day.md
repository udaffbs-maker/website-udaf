## SPORTS DAY 2024

# Event Details

**1\. Short-pitch cricket (only for males)**

- **Team members:** 10-12
- **Entry fee:** 1000 BDT
- **Date:** September 30, 2024, and October 1, 2024

 

2\. Carrom (for both  male and female)

- Team members: 2
- Entry fee: 150 BDT
- Date: October 1, 2024

 

3\. Ludo (only for females)

- Team members: 2
- Entry fee: 150 BDT
- Date: October 1, 2024
