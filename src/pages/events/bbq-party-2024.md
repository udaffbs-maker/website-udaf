## bbq party 2024

UDAF Executive Committee 2023 hosted the "**UDAF BBQ Party**", making it a memorable moment in the club's history. The event took place on the peaceful University of Dhaka campus. It portrayed the unity and friendship within the UDAF community. Magical fairy lights were used in the venue's design to create a vibrant and inviting ambiance for all attendees. Friendship, humor, and happiness were abundant at the celebration that brought together members, alumni, and the advisory panel. There were many enjoyable activities, such as lively singing sessions and exciting badminton matches. The delicious aroma of grilling filled the air, providing a wonderful break for our dedicated executives after they successfully managed two events in a row.

**Event Details:**

Date: 9th February 2024  
Location: University of Dhaka Campus  
Time: 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM  
Participation: 70 persons

https://youtu.be/ioyMzhnTk7o?si=Xt4AvuQMyHglfvYX

The UDAF BBQ Party was a refreshing break for our club members and a wonderful chance to reconnect with our valued alumni. It was also an opportunity to build a strong bonds by uniting current members, former members, and our esteemed advisory panel. We hoped to accelerate participation, forge enduring relationships, and obtain vital support for the continued progress and success of our beloved club through this vibrant event.
