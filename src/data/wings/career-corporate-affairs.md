##

![](/images/wings/career-corporate-affairs/Career-Corporate.svg)

Career and Corporate Affairs wing of UDAF is a more informal and conversational way where the magic happens, where the academia and corporate sector join forces to create some amazing opportunities for students.

First off, they are the masterminds behind our signature case competitions, like the famous 'Prodigies'. These competitions are not only thrilling but also provide a platform for the participants to showcase their skills and talents. The adrenaline rushes the individuals as they compete against their peers and work on real-world business cases. It's an incredible experience that will definitely level up one’s problem-solving and critical thinking abilities.

But that's not all! The Career and Corporate Affairs wing also arranges various skills and career sessions which are designed to be equipped with  knowledge and insights needed to excel in professional journeys. One has the opportunity to collaborate with industry experts and faculty members who will share their wisdom and experiences. It's like having a backstage pass to the secrets of success!

Being a part of this wing, individuals not only gain practical skills but also expand networks. Rubbing shoulders with professionals from different industries, which can open doors to internships, mentorships, and even job opportunities. Plus, the connections they make here can last a lifetime!

Inquisitiveness on exploring what happens behind the scenes of arranging the exciting events Career and Corporate Affairs wing of UDAF is the perfect place to be.
