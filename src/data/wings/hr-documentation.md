HR & Documentation wing basically deals with the people of UDAF, manages its members and helps them flourish with various skills and expertise. Besides, this wing performs as an archive of each and every activity conducted by UDAF.

The Human Resources and Documentation wing recruits UDAF members, hires manpower, builds up relations among the recruited personnel, manages their performances, trains up as well as maintains the whole administrative body. Joining HR can help a member be fluent in communication, problem-solving, negotiation, attention to detail etc. Skills like  leadership, adaptability, business acumen and so on, which furnishes one to cope up with this fast growing world, are ensured to gather from this HR wing. Moreover, HR provides an opportunity to learn continuously, face new challenges, get mentorship, learn time management and achieve personal and professional goals.

The Documentation part of this wing preserves each and every activity of UDAF in detail. It records the papers and documents of UDAF, goes through the meeting discussions, notes down the decisions and observes the activities of all UDAF members.

In wholesome, the HR and Documentation Wing offers to help the UDAF members grow up personally, gather professional skills as well as be a skilled human resource.
