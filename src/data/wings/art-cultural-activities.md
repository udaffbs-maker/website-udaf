##

![](/images/wings/art-cultural-activities/Arts-Culture.svg)

The Art and Cultural Wing of UDAF hosts various events every year, bringing a burst of creativity to the campus. **'UDAF Says Hi'** is a flagship program organized every year, where students dance, sing, recite poems, and perform dramas to welcome the freshers, creating a friendly atmosphere.

Beyond these events, the Cultural Wing provides an ongoing platform for creative expression. Dance performances showcase different styles, and singing talents add harmonies to the UDAF community. The stage becomes a dynamic space for drama and poetic recitations, adding artistic diversity.

In addition to performances, the Art and Cultural wing organizes sessions and workshops to help students improve their singing, dancing, and recitation skills. These opportunities contribute to individual growth and enhance the overall artistic atmosphere at UDAF.

Overall, the Art and Cultural Wing is more than just a showcase; it's a nurturing ground for creativity. Through dance, singing, workshops, and more, it forms together a lively cultural fabric that defines the spirit of UDAF.
