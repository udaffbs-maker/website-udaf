##

![](/images/wings/sports-entertainment/Sports-Entertainment.svg)

UDAF Sports & Entertainment wing organizes exciting cricket, football, badminton, and basketball matches every year. It's a place where students come together to enjoy sportsmanship and friendly competition. Badminton enthusiasts play on dedicated courts, while basketball games welcome both girls and boys, showcasing their teamwork and skills. BADminton PRO tournament is the flagship tournament organized by UDAF every year.

A major highlight is the annual cricket tournament, drawing cricket fans from the student community. Teams compete passionately, emphasizing not just victories but also the values of discipline and teamwork. Short pitch and long pitch tournaments are held frequently. These tournaments have become a tradition, forming a sense of community and shared joy among participants.

Teachers and alumni join the matches and create a unique connection across many batches.The UDAF Sports Wing aims to promote physical activity while creating a space for every student to participate. The success of these events lies in the memories created – be it intense matches, exciting goals, or graceful rallies.

As each tournament concludes, the UDAF Sports Wing stands as a symbol of athletic excellence and community bonding, where the love for sports brings people together.
